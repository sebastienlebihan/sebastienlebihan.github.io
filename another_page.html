Research and Projects

This page highlights some of the research projects I've worked on during my studies in astrophysics at the Observatoire de Paris.

Project 1: Understanding Dark Matter

This project focused on exploring the nature of dark matter and its influence on galaxy formation. Using data from the latest astronomical surveys, we aimed to refine existing models and propose new hypotheses about this mysterious substance.

Project 2: Exoplanet Detection Techniques

In this research, we investigated various methods for detecting exoplanets, including the transit method and radial velocity measurements. Our work contributed to the development of more accurate detection techniques, helping to discover new exoplanets.

Project 3: Stellar Evolution

This project involved studying the lifecycle of stars, particularly focusing on the later stages of stellar evolution. We analyzed data from several telescopes to better understand supernovae and neutron stars.

For more details on these and other projects, please feel free to reach out through the contact page.
